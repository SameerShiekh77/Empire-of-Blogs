Powerful Suction for Deep Cleaning

The DEEBOT T30 PRO OMNI boasts an impressive 11,000Pa suction power, making it perfect for deep carpet cleaning. Its new high-speed motor and straight-through duct design enhance cleaning efficiency, ensuring no dirt or debris is left behind. 

Edge-Mopping and Tangle-Free Technology 

This robot is the first of its kind to offer truly edge-mopping capabilities. It cleans right up to the edge of your walls with a precision of just 1mm, leaving no dead zones. Additionally, its V-shaped anti-static bristles significantly reduce hair tangling, providing a hassle-free cleaning experience.

Intelligent Obstacle Avoidance and Mapping 

Equipped with TrueDetect 3D 3.0 technology, the DEEBOT T30 PRO OMNI can detect and avoid obstacles with incredible accuracy. TrueMapping 2.0 technology allows the robot to map 100 square meters in just six minutes, ensuring thorough and efficient cleaning. 

Versatile Cleaning Capabilities 

The DEEBOT T30 PRO OMNI can handle various home environments with ease. It can conquer obstacles up to 20mm in height on both dry and wet surfaces. The 9mm auto-lift mopping system ensures that carpets are protected by lifting the mopping plates when a carpet is detected. 

Hands-Free Convenience 

The Mini OMNI Station, a space-efficient docking station, provides a range of features including auto-empty, one-tap self-cleaning, and auto-refill. This design not only saves space but also minimizes noise, ensuring a quieter cleaning experience. 

Voice Assistant Compatibility
